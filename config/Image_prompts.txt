MASTER IMAGE PROMPTS — 4 FINANCIAL NEWS POSTER TEMPLATES

============================================================ PROMPT 1 —
STOCK MARKET HOLIDAY / IMPORTANT MARKET UPDATE
============================================================

Create a premium vertical financial news poster for an Indian stock
market social media page.

FORMAT: Vertical portrait poster, 2:3 aspect ratio,
ultra-high-resolution, cinematic financial editorial design, optimized
for Instagram and LinkedIn.

OVERALL STYLE: Premium Indian financial news media aesthetic. Dark
black, charcoal, metallic gold, warm amber and selective white color
palette. High contrast cinematic lighting. Professional, trustworthy and
authoritative. The design should feel like a combination of a premium
financial magazine cover, institutional market research publication and
cinematic stock market poster.

LAYOUT:

TOP HEADER: Create a clean premium masthead area.

Place a minimal candlestick-chart inspired brand icon on the upper left.

Brand name: “[BRAND NAME]”

Tagline below: “[BRAND TAGLINE]”

Use elegant condensed sans-serif typography. Keep the header
sophisticated and uncluttered.

MAIN LEFT HEADLINE: Use approximately 40% of the upper composition for a
very large stacked headline.

Headline: “[MAIN HEADLINE LINE 1] [MAIN HEADLINE LINE 2] [MAIN HEADLINE
LINE 3]”

Example visual hierarchy: STOCK MARKET HOLIDAY

Use bold condensed uppercase typography. First two lines in textured
off-white. Most important keyword in strong financial gold/yellow.

RIGHT-SIDE HERO VISUAL: Create a photorealistic cinematic representation
of [EVENT / FESTIVAL / REASON FOR HOLIDAY].

Example visual possibilities: - grand mosque architecture for Muharram
or Eid related closure - Diwali lamps and exchange district skyline -
Gandhi silhouette or monument for Gandhi Jayanti - Indian flag and
government architecture for national holidays

The architecture and cultural symbolism should be respectful, elegant
and realistic.

Use dramatic split lighting: dark stormy atmosphere on one side, warm
golden sunlight on the other side.

INFORMATION CARD: Add a premium black rectangular information card with
thin gold border.

Text hierarchy: “[EXCHANGE NAMES] WILL REMAIN” “[STATUS]” “[DATE OR
DAY]”

Make STATUS the largest word in bright gold.

Reserve a clean lower section for verified exchange logos to be added
later in post-production.

HANGING STATUS BOARD: On the right side, add a realistic premium hanging
market signboard suspended by thick gold ropes.

Board text: “[MARKET STATUS]” “[FULL DATE]”

Example: MARKET CLOSED 26 JUNE 2026

The board should look physically realistic with: matte black surface,
subtle gold border, metallic details, cinematic shadows, realistic rope
tension.

DATE SECTION: Create a calendar icon followed by:

“[DATE]” “[DAY]”

Use white for date and gold for day.

EVENT REASON: Add a small symbolic icon representing the occasion.

Text: “ON THE OCCASION OF” “[EVENT NAME]” “[YEAR]”

Make EVENT NAME prominent in gold.

BOTTOM FINANCIAL VISUAL: Show a realistic bronze bull and bear facing
each other.

Behind them: subtle green and red candlestick chart, dark trading floor
atmosphere, reflections on polished black floor, soft market ticker
glow.

BOTTOM CTA: Add a small premium information box: “[SHORT CTA]”

Example: PLAN YOUR TRADES, STAY AHEAD.

At the bottom: “FOLLOW FOR MORE”

Then three small categories: “DAILY UPDATES | STOCK MARKET INSIGHTS |
SMART INVESTING”

COMPOSITION RULES: Clear visual hierarchy. Do not overcrowd. Keep all
important text within safe margins. Use strong grid alignment. Maintain
premium spacing. No random decorative elements. No cartoon graphics. No
cheap template appearance. No excessive neon. No fake or distorted
logos. No spelling errors. No repeated words. No malformed architecture.
No extra text.

FINAL LOOK: Cinematic, premium, institutional, trustworthy, Indian
financial media poster, photorealistic, editorial advertising quality,
dramatic but credible.

============================================================ PROMPT 2 —
BREAKING GEOPOLITICAL NEWS / OIL / TRADE ROUTE STORY
============================================================

Create a high-impact vertical BREAKING NEWS poster about a major
geopolitical development affecting global trade, crude oil, shipping
routes or financial markets.

FORMAT: Vertical portrait poster, 2:3 aspect ratio,
ultra-high-resolution, cinematic editorial news photography style.

TOP BREAKING NEWS BANNER: Create a bold horizontal banner across the
top.

Left section: Red background with white italic uppercase text:
“BREAKING”

Right section: Yellow background with black italic uppercase text:
“NEWS”

The banner should resemble a professional television financial-news
alert, with strong typography and clean spacing.

MAIN SCENE: Create a dramatic photorealistic aerial coastal scene
representing:

“[LOCATION / STRATEGIC WATERWAY / COUNTRY]”

Show: - rugged coastal mountains, - deep blue strategic shipping
channel, - multiple realistic commercial oil tankers, - cargo ships
moving through the water, - visible shipping traffic, - realistic
industrial port infrastructure, - distant radar or communications
infrastructure.

The scene should communicate economic importance and geopolitical
tension without looking like a fictional war movie.

NATIONAL CONTEXT: Include a large realistic flag of: “[COUNTRY]”

The flag should be naturally positioned in the environment and correctly
proportioned.

GEOPOLITICAL TENSION ELEMENTS: In the distant sky, show a limited number
of realistic military aircraft.

Important: Aircraft should be secondary visual elements. Do not
overcrowd the sky. Maintain realistic scale and atmospheric perspective.

POLICY / TOLL VISUAL METAPHOR: Create a realistic checkpoint or
toll-gate structure integrated into the port or shipping environment.

Signboard text: “[SHORT POLICY LABEL]”

Example: IRAN TOLL

The toll structure should visually communicate: shipping fees, transit
charges, strategic control, commercial restrictions.

Do not make the structure look cartoonish.

FOREGROUND LEADER / OFFICIAL: Place a large editorial-style portrait of
a relevant political leader or government official in the lower-right
foreground.

Expression: serious, focused, statesmanlike.

Lighting: cinematic rim light, natural skin texture, soft depth of
field, subtle integration with the background.

If exact identity is not essential, use a generic senior government
official rather than imitating a specific real person.

MAIN HEADLINE AREA: Create a dark black-to-transparent gradient across
the lower 35% of the poster for readability.

Add a large multi-line uppercase headline:

“[HEADLINE LINE 1] [HEADLINE LINE 2] [HEADLINE LINE 3] [HEADLINE LINE
4]”

Example hierarchy: IRAN CONSIDERS CHARGING TAXES AND TOLL FEES TO CROSS
THE STRAIT OF HORMUZ

Highlight the most important phrases in amber-orange or gold.

Use: white for supporting words, orange-gold for key economic or
geopolitical terms.

TYPOGRAPHY: Extra-bold geometric sans-serif. Very high readability.
Large mobile-friendly text. Strong line spacing. No thin fonts. No text
overlapping the face or important ships.

MOOD: Urgent but credible. Serious financial-news tone. Global trade
disruption atmosphere. Cinematic realism. Bright blue sky contrasted
with geopolitical tension. Professional newsroom composition.

NEGATIVE INSTRUCTIONS: No fake maps. No incorrect flags. No excessive
explosions. No fantasy military equipment. No random soldiers. No text
clutter. No distorted ships. No duplicated aircraft. No malformed faces.
No unreadable text. No spelling mistakes. No sensational gore. No
cartoon styling.

FINAL LOOK: Premium global financial-news thumbnail, geopolitical
editorial photography combined with modern television breaking-news
graphics, cinematic realism, high contrast, professional editorial
composition.

============================================================ PROMPT 3 —
CORPORATE FRAUD / STOCK CRASH / INVESTIGATIVE STORY
============================================================

Create a dark cinematic investigative financial documentary poster about
a major corporate fraud, accounting scandal, regulatory investigation or
stock market collapse.

FORMAT: Vertical portrait poster, 2:3 aspect ratio,
ultra-high-resolution. Dark investigative documentary aesthetic. Premium
financial thriller visual language. Designed for Instagram, LinkedIn and
YouTube Shorts cover.

COLOR PALETTE: Deep black, charcoal, blood red, dark crimson, dirty
newspaper beige, selective white typography, subtle amber city lights.

OVERALL MOOD: Serious. Investigative. Financial destruction. Corporate
secrecy. Investor losses. Market panic. Documentary credibility.

TOP-LEFT MAIN TITLE: Create a huge stacked uppercase title:

“[COMPANY OR CASE NAME]” “[EVENT TYPE]”

Example: RAJESH EXPORT SCAM

Use distressed condensed typography.

Company/case name: off-white textured lettering.

Event type: deep red distressed lettering.

The headline should dominate the upper-left area.

RIGHT-SIDE CORPORATE VISUAL: Show a dramatic low-angle view of:

“[CORPORATE BUILDING / STOCK EXCHANGE BUILDING / FINANCIAL DISTRICT]”

Use: dark stormy sky, heavy clouds, orange city lights, wet street
reflections, ominous nighttime atmosphere, dramatic perspective.

If a real building is not essential, create a generic financial exchange
tower rather than falsely representing a specific location.

STOCK COLLAPSE GRAPH: On the left side, integrate a large red
candlestick chart falling sharply downward.

Include: multiple declining red candles, steep downward arrow,
percentage loss: “[DECLINE %]”

Example: -79.80%

The graph should look visually dramatic but clean.

Do not create fake numerical market data beyond the values explicitly
provided.

CENTRAL INVESTIGATIVE SUBHEAD: Place a strong stacked subheadline in the
center:

“HOW IT” “[IMPACT WORD]” “INVESTOR” “WEALTH”

Example: HOW IT DESTROYED INVESTOR WEALTH

Use alternating white and red typography.

Make the emotional impact word and WEALTH red.

BOTTOM EVIDENCE TABLE: Create an investigative desk scene with
overlapping financial newspapers and case files.

Include three major documents:

Document 1: Company name: “[COMPANY NAME]”

Large red rubber stamp: “[CASE LABEL]”

Example: SCAM FRAUD UNDER INVESTIGATION

Include a small falling share-price graph.

Document 2: Newspaper headline: “[INVESTIGATION HEADLINE]”

Example: AGENCIES PROBE ALLEGED ₹6,000 CRORE FRAUD

Document 3: Newspaper headline: “[INVESTOR IMPACT HEADLINE]”

Example: INVESTORS LOSE CRORES

IMPORTANT: All allegations must be worded accurately. If the matter is
under investigation, use: “ALLEGED” “UNDER INVESTIGATION” “PROBE” or
other legally accurate language.

Do not visually present an unproven allegation as a final conviction.

EVIDENCE PROPS: Add subtle investigative objects: - calculator, -
financial statements, - paper clips, - audit papers, - handwritten
calculation marks, - stock chart printouts.

Keep them realistic and secondary.

BOTTOM CTA BUTTON: Add a glowing dark-red documentary-style button:

“[CTA TEXT]”

Example: THE REAL STORY WHAT REALLY HAPPENED THE FULL BREAKDOWN

Use white serif or bold editorial text.

LIGHTING: Dark cinematic lighting. Red glow from falling chart. Warm
building lights. Subtle rain reflections. Strong contrast. Deep shadows.
Documentary thriller atmosphere.

TYPOGRAPHY: Large condensed uppercase fonts. Distressed texture only for
main title. Clean bold sans-serif for explanatory text. Strong
readability on mobile screens.

COMPOSITION: Top 40% = case identity and corporate visual. Middle 25% =
stock crash and investor impact. Bottom 35% = documentary evidence and
newspapers.

NEGATIVE INSTRUCTIONS: No random fake statistics. No incorrect company
logos. No fake regulator logos. No meaningless newspaper text. No
excessive blood or violence imagery. No cartoon graphics. No crypto
aesthetic. No cyberpunk aesthetic. No distorted buildings. No duplicated
objects. No malformed typography. No cheap clickbait arrows everywhere.
No exaggerated claims beyond supplied facts.

FINAL LOOK: Premium investigative finance documentary poster, dark
corporate thriller aesthetic, cinematic realism, institutional financial
storytelling, dramatic stock market collapse visualization,
sophisticated and credible.

============================================================ PROMPT 4 —
INDIAN DEFENCE SECTOR / DEFENCE STOCKS RALLY
============================================================

Create a powerful vertical financial-news poster focused on the Indian
defence sector, defence manufacturing, military modernization, defence
orders, government policy, exports, or a rally in Indian defence stocks.

FORMAT: Vertical portrait poster, 2:3 aspect ratio,
ultra-high-resolution, cinematic editorial finance poster, optimized for
Instagram, LinkedIn, X and YouTube Shorts cover.

OVERALL VISUAL STYLE: Premium Indian financial-news documentary
aesthetic. Cinematic military-industrial atmosphere. Powerful, serious
and authoritative. A combination of defence technology, national
strategic strength and stock-market storytelling. Photorealistic,
dramatic and editorial — not a video-game poster.

COLOR PALETTE: Deep black and charcoal shadows, military olive and
gunmetal grey, warm orange sunset, selective saffron, white and green
details, bright financial yellow for important headline words,
controlled red glow only for the bottom CTA.

BACKGROUND AND SKY: Create a dramatic sunset sky with dense cinematic
clouds. Use warm orange sunlight breaking through dark blue-grey clouds.
Add subtle smoke, dust and atmospheric haze near the ground. The
atmosphere should feel powerful and strategic, but not depict active
destruction or civilian harm.

TOP-LEFT NATIONAL SYMBOL: Place a large, realistic Indian national flag
waving strongly in the wind.

Requirements: Correct saffron, white and green horizontal bands. Correct
navy-blue Ashoka Chakra centered in the white band. Natural fabric folds
and realistic wind movement. Strong cinematic rim lighting. The flag
should occupy the upper-left background without covering the headline.

TOP-RIGHT AIR POWER VISUAL: Show one modern Indian military fighter
aircraft flying dynamically upward across the upper-right sky.

Requirements: Realistic scale and aerodynamic proportions. Indian Air
Force roundel visible where appropriate. Cinematic sunlight reflecting
from the aircraft body. Dynamic but believable flight angle. No missiles
being fired. No explosions. No duplicated aircraft unless the story
specifically requires a formation.

MAIN HEADLINE: Use the left-middle portion of the poster for a huge
stacked headline.

Text: “[MAIN HEADLINE LINE 1]” “[MAIN HEADLINE LINE 2]” “[MAIN HEADLINE
LINE 3]”

Example: INDIAN DEFENCE SECTOR

Typography: Ultra-bold condensed uppercase sans-serif. Slight distressed
print texture. First and third lines in textured off-white. Most
important sector keyword in strong financial yellow or gold.

The headline must be extremely readable on a mobile screen. Use strong
spacing and alignment. Do not overlap the aircraft or major defence
equipment.

RIGHT-SIDE DEFENCE INDUSTRIAL COMPOSITION: Create a layered
photorealistic military-industrial scene showing selected Indian defence
capabilities relevant to the news story.

Possible elements: - modern battle tank in the lower-right foreground, -
missile defence system or surface-to-air missile launchers in the
midground, - large radar system in the right background, - fighter
aircraft in the sky, - subtle defence manufacturing facility
silhouettes, - optional naval vessel if the story concerns shipbuilding
or naval orders.

IMPORTANT: Use only 3–4 major defence elements. Do not overcrowd the
composition. Maintain realistic scale and depth. Equipment should look
credible and technically coherent. Avoid fantasy weapons or impossible
combinations.

FOREGROUND TANK: Place a large modern battle tank in the lower-right
foreground.

Visual requirements: Low-angle cinematic perspective. Heavy metallic
details. Realistic tracks, armour plates, optics and turret. Subtle
Indian tricolour identification marking where visually appropriate. Dust
and atmospheric particles around the tracks. Warm sunset highlights on
metal surfaces. Tank barrel directed safely away from the viewer. No
firing or explosions.

MISSILE AND RADAR SECTION: Behind the tank, create a vertical defence
technology composition.

Include: 2–3 realistic missile canisters or interceptors, a large
phased-array radar unit, military support structures in the background.

If the story is about a specific verified defence system, visually
represent that system accurately. If accuracy cannot be guaranteed, use
a generic modern Indian air-defence system rather than incorrect labels.

SECONDARY QUESTION / EXPLAINER HEADLINE: In the lower-left section,
create a smaller stacked question:

“[QUESTION LINE 1]” “[QUESTION LINE 2]” “[QUESTION LINE 3]”

Example: WHY DEFENCE STOCKS RALLYING?

Typography hierarchy: “WHY” in bold white. “DEFENCE” in bold white.
“STOCKS” in bright financial yellow. “RALLYING?” in bright financial
yellow.

Use clean bold condensed typography. Keep this secondary headline
significantly smaller than the main sector title.

OPTIONAL MARKET CONTEXT: If relevant to the story, subtly integrate: -
faint upward candlestick chart, - soft green market line, - small
order-book document silhouettes, - defence contract papers, - export
shipment silhouettes, - factory lights.

These elements must remain subtle and should not compete with the
defence visuals.

BOTTOM CTA: Create a large premium glowing red rectangular button with
rounded corners and a bright red neon border.

CTA text: “[CTA TEXT]”

Example: REAL TRUTH FULL STORY WHY STOCKS ARE RISING THE REAL REASON

Use huge bold white uppercase typography. The CTA must be readable
instantly but should not look like a cheap clickbait button.

LIGHTING: Strong cinematic sunset backlight. Warm orange rim light on
aircraft, missiles, radar and tank. Deep shadows in lower sections.
Subtle dust particles and sparks in the atmosphere. High contrast
without crushing all shadow detail. Realistic metal reflections.
Volumetric light rays through clouds.

COMPOSITION STRUCTURE: Upper 25%: Indian flag + dramatic sky + fighter
aircraft.

Middle 45%: Huge main headline on left + missiles/radar on right.

Lower 30%: Secondary explainer question on left + tank on right + CTA
near bottom.

TEXT SAFE AREA: Keep all text within safe margins. No headline touching
the edge. No text hidden behind equipment. Ensure visual hierarchy
remains clear even on a small mobile display.

EDITORIAL ACCURACY RULES: Do not imply that all defence stocks are
rising unless verified. Do not invent defence orders, contract values,
export figures or government announcements. Do not use the logo or exact
product name of a defence company unless supplied and verified. If the
story concerns one company, visually focus on the relevant defence
segment rather than implying the entire sector moved. Use accurate
language such as: “DEFENCE STOCKS RALLY” “SELECT DEFENCE STOCKS GAIN”
“NEW ORDER BOOST” “EXPORT MOMENTUM” “POLICY PUSH” only when supported by
the supplied story data.

NEGATIVE INSTRUCTIONS: No war destruction. No civilian casualties. No
blood or gore. No active missile launch toward a target. No explosions
unless the verified story specifically concerns combat footage and such
imagery is editorially necessary. No fantasy aircraft. No impossible
weapon systems. No incorrect national flag. No malformed Ashoka Chakra.
No random military logos. No fake company logos. No invented stock
prices. No fake percentage gains. No duplicated tanks. No malformed
wheels or tracks. No distorted aircraft wings. No excessive sparks. No
video-game aesthetic. No cartoon appearance. No cheap template design.
No excessive text. No spelling mistakes. No meaningless labels on
missiles. No unnecessary patriotic slogans.

FINAL LOOK: Premium Indian defence-sector financial news poster,
cinematic realism, powerful sunset lighting, strategic
military-industrial visual storytelling, high-impact mobile readability,
institutional finance-meets-defence editorial aesthetic, dramatic but
credible, sophisticated and professional.
